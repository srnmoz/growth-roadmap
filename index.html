Ad hoc and cross-team work guardrail

Team 1 will operate on a 70/30 capacity model: about 70% for planned roadmap work and up to 30% for high-priority ad hoc requests from Product, Engineering, Partnerships, Brand, Agency, events, launches, leadership asks, and other Growth teams.

Ad hoc work must directly support ARR contribution, acquisition, visibility, conversion, authority, or urgent risk reduction. Requests beyond the 30% buffer are not absorbed silently; they are queued, traded off against existing roadmap work, or escalated for leadership reprioritisation.

What we stop. Net-new blog volume for its own sake · sitewide optimisation passes · unscoped support work · zombie experiments with no metric or kill date.
